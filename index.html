Automatic Testing

I have expertise in software automation and QA, including scripting with Cypress, Playwright, Selenium and Appium for automated and mobile testing. I handle API validation with Postman and Insomnia, request simulation with MITM and Proxyman, and database verification using VPN. I also have experience in performance testing with JMeter, functional testing such as regression, end-to-end and integration, and BDD strategies with Cucumber. In addition, I participate in design validations with Figma and elaborate detailed bug handling reports in Jira, using RTM to ensure full coverage.

Manual Testing

Throughout my career, I have greatly appreciated the importance of testing in software development, which ensures that the final product meets the established requirements.
